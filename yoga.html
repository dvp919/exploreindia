Yoga And Wellnes

Retreat, Recharge, Rejuvenate

From yoga ashrams to spas and wellness centres, India offers a host of sites.

Destination :-

Rishikesh

First up on this list is a place known as the "Yoga capital of the world," and it is every serious yogi's dream to visit Rishikesh.

Auroville

Auroville is home to the Integral yoga community and the teachings of Sri Aurobindo, a revered figure in the yoga community.

Dharamshala

Dharamsala served as the place of refuge for the Dalai Lama who fled religious persecution in Tibet.

Goa

Tourists in Goa can experience southern India's herbal medicinal practices and experience ayurvedic (whole-body) medicine first-hand.

Kerala

Yogis who visit Kerala will find peace of mind when practicing their art and will be able to experience traditional Indian cultures first hand.

Gokarna

Gokarna is home to incredibly fantastic yoga schools and institutions and houses numerous ashrams, meditation centers, and retreat locations.

Resorts :-

Resort Name Place Speciality
Ananda in the Himalayas Rishikesh The prime Specialization of Ananda is the spa. They have more than 80 varieties of spa therapies which are the mixture of traditional Ayurveda with modern spa.
Himalaya Shanti Ashram Dharamshala This Centre is located in a small mountain village surrounded by forests. The surroundings here are quieter and much more serene. They have this belief that being closer to nature is the perfect environment for practising this sacred art.
The Mandala Resort Goa The Mandala Resort is a tranquil oasis within which to enjoy a luxurious yoga retreat or holiday. Their holistic approach to wellness touches all of mind, body, and spirit as they offer both yoga retreats and fasting programs.
Devaaya Goa One can prefer the center for Ayurveda treatment and therapies for joint pains, rheumatism, arthritis, diabetes, spondylitis, and partial paralysis. Panchakarma and wellness treatments are the highlights of Ayurveda treatment in Devaaya.
Carnoustie Ayurveda and Wellness Resort Kerala Extensive gardens growing Ayurvedic herbs, yoga, meditation, and chanting hall, well-stocked library, music and cultural performances, and a house boat available for relaxing cruises on the Punnamada and Vembanad Lakes.
SwaSwara Resort Gokarna Nature trails and bird watching, boat rides, village visits, interactive cooking classes, art studio, large oxidized swimming pool, meditation deck on the cliff, and yoga and meditation hut.
